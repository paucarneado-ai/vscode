Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 on the Protection of Personal Data and the guarantee of digital rights (LOPD-GDD), we inform you that the personal data you provide will be processed by:

  • Controller: SentYacht
  • Address: Port Esportiu, Local 49, El Masnou, 08320, Barcelona, Spain
  • Email: jordi@sentyacht.es
  • Phone: +34 609 865 215

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The personal data collected through the Website forms will be processed for the following purposes:

  • Managing enquiries and information requests made by users regarding boats.
  • Sending commercial information related to our boat brokerage services, with prior user consent.
  • Maintaining commercial relationships and providing requested services.
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

The legal basis for processing your personal data is:

  • Consent: by submitting a contact or information request form, the user expressly consents to the processing of their data for the stated purpose.
  • Performance of a contract: when processing is necessary for the provision of a service requested by the user.
  • Legitimate interest: for improving our services and fraud prevention.
  • Legal obligation: for compliance with applicable legal obligations.

4. Personal Data Collected

The personal data we may collect through our Website includes:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User message or enquiry
  • Browsing data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

5. Data Retention Period

Personal data will be retained for the time necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected and to determine any liabilities arising from said purpose. Data will be retained in accordance with legally established periods.

6. Data Recipients

Personal data will not be disclosed to third parties, except where required by law. Where necessary, data may be communicated to:

  • Competent public authorities, in cases provided for by law.
  • Service providers acting as data processors, with whom appropriate data processing agreements have been executed.

7. Data Subject Rights

You may exercise the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access: to know whether your personal data is being processed and obtain a copy thereof.
  • Right of rectification: to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right of erasure: to request the deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary.
  • Right to object: to object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right of restriction: to request the restriction of the processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please contact jordi@sentyacht.es with a copy of your identity document.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (www.aepd.es) if you believe the processing of your data does not comply with applicable regulations.

8. Security Measures

SentYacht has implemented the necessary technical and organisational measures to ensure the security of personal data and to prevent alteration, loss, unauthorised processing, or access, taking into account the state of technology, the nature of the stored data, and the risks to which they are exposed.
