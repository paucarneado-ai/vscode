Not all vessels should be sold the same way

Nor do all brokers represent the same standard.

When an owner decides to sell a vessel of value, they need far more than exposure. They need judgement, positioning, market knowledge, and professional representation capable of protecting both the vessel and the perception of its worth.

At Sent-Yacht, that is precisely our philosophy. We offer a premium yacht brokerage service for owners who seek serious, discreet and carefully managed representation, with direct attention and a commercial vision built on decades of real experience in the industry.

The career of Jordi Sentíes, which began in 1976, provides Sent-Yacht with a solid foundation of knowledge, relationships, and deep understanding of the nautical market. That experience, combined with a personal and rigorous approach, ensures every transaction is handled with the professionalism a quality vessel demands.

Based in El Masnou, with an operational presence in Barcelona and other Mediterranean ports, we represent vessels with the level of excellence that a specialised brand and a discerning clientele deserve.

If you wish to sell your boat with confidence, professionalism, and representation that matches its value, contact Sent-Yacht.
